Why Anime

Japanese Animation

Japanese animation, more commonly referred to as “anime”, has been a popular genre of an artistic style of storytelling originating as far back as the early 1900's. Although Japanese animation has always had influence in the Americas, it has exploded in popularity since 2020, largely due to quarantining as a result of COVID 19. When the world entered a social distancing period and underwent a lockdown, many of us turned to technology and media in order to keep ourselves entertained, discovering new forms of entertainment. One such form of entertainment was anime. As you navigate this website, hopefully we are able to educate you on what exactly anime is and why you WILL want to watch it.
